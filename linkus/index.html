What is LinkUs

LINK US WITH LOVE

LinkUs is a web portal designed to promote social connectedness amongst the elderly in Malaysia. Also an application which can help to strengthen family bonding, especially to show more loves and cares to the senior family members. Besides, this application will also include a significant feature to help notify other family members whenever detect bad health wellness, elderlyâ€™s indoor safety and less frequent contact.

Our Objectives

To encourage social connectedness among the elderly with family members.

To promote health connectedness of the elderly with family members by monitor health wellness and safety.

To enhance emotional connectedness between the elderly and family members.

Features

Abnormal Sound Detection & Alert System

Abnormal sound detection approach is expected to monitor elderly's indoor safety or hazards, once occurrence detected, an alert message will be sent to other members.

Family Connectedness Circle

A connectivity graphic will depict how strong the elderly are being connected with other family members based on interaction.

Health Wellness Indicator

After taking account of different health aspects, a radar chart expected to indicate the total healthscore of the elderly.

Emotion Detection

It is just a simple and entertaining feature used to capture user's emotion then respond accordingly.

Who Are We

We are a team of four undergraduates from FCSIT University of Malaya under supervision of Associate Prof. Dr. Chiew Thiam Kian to work on our Innovative Product Project Initiative (IPPI) academic project. Currently, we are in collaboration with UM Connected Health & Fitness (UMCH) as a goal to develop and commercialize our final product. Our first intention is to create a platform which can link us closer to the elderly in our families as well as to encourage their social engagement.
