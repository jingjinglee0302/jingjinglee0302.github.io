What is Carenet

We CARE We CONNECT

Carenet is a web portal designed to promote social connectedness amongst the elderly in Malaysia. Also an application which can help to strengthen family bonding, especially to show more loves and cares to the senior family members. Besides, this application will also include a significant feature to help notify other family members whenever detect bad health wellness, elderlyâ€™s fall and less frequent contact.

Our Objectives

To promote social connectedness amongst the elderly.

To help monitoring health wellness of the elderly in the family.

To strengthen bonding and enhance relationship among the family especially for the senior family members.

Features

Falling Detection & Alert System

Deviceless-based approach is expected to detect falling incident, once occurrence detected, an alert message will be sent to other members .

Family Connectedness Circle

A circle will depict how strong the elderly are being connected with other family members based on interaction.

Health Wellness Indicator

After taking account of different health aspects, a radar chart expected to indicate the total healthscore of the elderly.

Simple Mood Detection

It is just a simple and entertaining feature used to capture user's mood then respond accordingly.

Who Are We

We are a team of four undergraduates from FCSIT University of Malaya under supervision of Dr. Chiew Thiam Kian to work on our IPPI project. Currently, we are in collaboration with UMCH as a goal to develop and commercialize our final product. Our first intention is to create a platform which can link us closer to the elderly in our families as well as to encourage their social engagement.
