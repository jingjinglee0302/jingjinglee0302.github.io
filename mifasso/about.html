About

Mifasso Music School

Mifasso Music Academy ® is a standard, modern and well-equipped virtual music school with professional team of musicians and vocalists. Our business aim is primarily to provide a new learning experience for people who want to learn music by offering online courses for a wide range of musical instrument and vocal music. We train people to master basic and advanced skills in playing musical instrument and vocal music through high-quality tutorial videos.

Our professional approach and continual development has led us to becoming the leading provider of professional music education. We combine innovative, fun and creative ways to teach music and engage with students to reach their full potential. Innovative music lessons and classes from beginner through advanced for children, teens, and adults. We are the best music school for you. We have programs to suit everyone’s needs.

We are the only professional music school offering lessons for children, adults and seniors.

Products & Services

Mifasso Music School

Mifasso Music Academy ® offers varieties of musical instruments online trainings and related services within the scope of the online music business industry, headquartered in Malaysia. Our intention of establishing this business via web platform is to help every individual to learn music, play different musical instruments in easier and more convenient way. Everyone can learn at home, room or dorm. We will always ensure to make profits ethically and we will do all that is permitted by the local law to achieve our aim and business goal. Our service offerings are listed below:

    Online courses
      Providing instructions and tutorials on music and how to play various musical instruments
    Customized Recommendation
      Recommend physical music school branches based on trainee’s preferences like residential area, transport, affordable tuition fee range, etc.
    Merchandise sales
      Offer online ordering and purchase of musical instruments, equipment and other accessories
    Other related services
      Online registration, schedule-checking and e-payment services, customer support service etc
