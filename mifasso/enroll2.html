Private lessons

Private music lessons provide one-on-one attention, so teachers can focus on an individual student’s needs.Private lessons help to minimize frustration. We believe the less frustration the better when it comes to learning; who doesn’t? Though frustration can create determination in some people, it can discourage others. It’s sad to see a child give up learning an instrument in the early stages when all was needed was a little personal attention. Every child deserves the opportunity to enjoy and not be frustrated with making music.
Mifasso private lessons is always designed for you success. With a private instructor, one can learn more quickly the fundamentals of playing. A solid foundation is built, it is easier to keep learning, playing and succeeding. Succeeding is fun! The young musician builds confidence, and doesn’t give up. Furthermore, it is easier to stay motivated with the help of our private instructor.
